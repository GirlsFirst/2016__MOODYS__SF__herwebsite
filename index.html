Hello World!

The mission of ImNotHer is to educate and empower.

Let's stop the stereotypes that keep girls out of computer science classes. Despite efforts to recruit women, the gender gaps in the fields collectively known as STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics) is not getting any better. The gaps in Computer Science and Engineering is the largest of the STEM discipline. Women are missing out on great jobs and the world is missing out on the amazing inventions women could be making in new technology. However, it is up to us, young women all across the globe, to defeat the stigma and reach beyond society's standards.
