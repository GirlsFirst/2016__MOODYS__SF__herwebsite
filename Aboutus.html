About Us

Our Vision

We're an San Francisco design team of five girls that believe in having a good time while doing what we love. Our mission is to empower girls to be exactly who they are. By providing knowledge and tools we hope to give females around the world the opportunity that they deserve, to reach beyond what they give themselves credit for. Sometimes girls need a little push to remember Iamnother.
